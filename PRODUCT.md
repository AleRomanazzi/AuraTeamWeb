# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small and mid-sized businesses, event organizers, and local brands across Argentina that need digital presence, production, or communication — social media management, audiovisual coverage, web development, or all three. They land on this site while evaluating agencies. They should feel impressed by the quality of the work, trust that AuraTeam understands their world, and be moved to make contact.

## Product Purpose

AuraTeam is a Córdoba-based creative and digital agency. This site is their primary sales tool: it communicates what they do, shows who they are, and converts visitors into clients. Success means a visitor finishes scrolling feeling confident enough to send a message or tap the WhatsApp button.

## Brand Personality

Electric, confident, human.

AuraTeam is young, local, and genuinely skilled — not a global agency template. The energy comes from craft and momentum, not noise. Tone is direct and approachable: knowledgeable without being cold, bold without being aggressive. The brand speaks Argentine Spanish without apology.

## Anti-references

- **Generic corporate blue** — safe navy + white + stock photography. Looks like every SaaS or enterprise landing page. No one feels anything.
- **Cheap / template-feeling** — WordPress-style generic agency sites with padded cards and rounded icons above every heading. Looks like it cost $50.
- **Overly dark / grungy** — industrial or heavy-metal darkness. Too aggressive for a team that works with pharmacies, fitness studios, and doctors.
- **Overly minimal / cold** — stark Swiss restraint that feels distant. This is a human team; the design should feel like it too.

## Design Principles

1. **Energy through quality, not volume.** Vibrancy comes from bold typography, decisive motion, and committed color — not from piling on effects or noise.
2. **Show, then tell.** The portfolio and client roster are the pitch; copy exists to frame the evidence, not to replace it.
3. **Local identity, professional reach.** The site should feel unmistakably Argentine and Córdoba-rooted — specific, not generic. Global aesthetics dilute that.
4. **Motion is a first-class material.** Transitions, reveals, and interactions are part of the design, not a finishing coat.
5. **Specificity builds trust.** Named clients, real numbers, actual team members. Vague claims ("world-class") are invisible; specific ones ("12+ brands worked") land.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA baseline: body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast, large text ≥ 3:1, keyboard navigation on all interactive elements, meaningful alt text on all portfolio and team images. `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` alternatives for every entrance animation.
